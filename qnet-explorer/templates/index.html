Why QNet?

Quantum Resistant

Built from the ground up with post-quantum cryptography to protect your assets against future quantum computer attacks.

Mobile First

Run a full node from your phone with minimal resources. Participate in consensus without expensive hardware.

Fair Distribution

No premining, no ICO. All coins are distributed fairly through mining over a 10-year period.

Developer Friendly

Comprehensive API and SDKs make it easy to build on top of QNet.

Decentralized Identity

Built-in DID support allows for secure, verifiable digital identities.

Punk Philosophy

Created by an individual developer, not a corporation. True to the original spirit of crypto.
